# Zettelkasten-styled Card Box View

## 1 What Is the Card Box

> 💡**Zettelkasten-styled card box —** the core area for managing all your note cards in one place:
> 
> In the card box, learners can view, search, trace back, and edit every note card.
> 
> More importantly, the card box opens up the connections between a study set and multiple mind maps, letting notes, documents, and mind maps work in tandem. This enables cross-document, cross-study-set knowledge integration and review, so your knowledge system keeps growing as cards flow through it.
> 
> ![Card box in action Card box in action ](image/0f6e33fceca81b10.webp)

## 2 Opening the Card Box

- **In non-fullscreen mode**, tap the `Cards` button in the top-right corner of the study set to open the `Zettelkasten-styled card box`.
- **In fullscreen mode**, tap `...` (Study Set – More) in the top-right corner of the study set, then the `Cards` button to open the `Zettelkasten-styled card box`.

### 3.2 Operations on the Card Itself (Edit / Backlink)

![Action buttons appear after tapping a card Action buttons appear after tapping a card ](image/30b30ada8ba97211.webp)

![Action buttons in the small-screen mind map Action buttons in the small-screen mind map ](image/123402d39bb71438.webp)

- Tap the **blank part of the card** to bring up the action panel:
  1. Edit card content. See: [Mind Map Cards ①: Creating and Editing Cards](../67hDfmy3SZi4oXmMb2bvcH/index.md)
  2. Tap the mind map location to open the `card grouping board` (see [Card Grouping Board ①: Precisely Filtering a Huge Pool of Cards](../dJvh1K4GEKaJYtZ14zbfgj/index.md))
     1. Switch the card source (can be study set / mind map / document)
     2. Apply primary and secondary filtering
     3. Jump to the fullscreen mind map
        > ❗Can't backlink to the location? Check `Study Set – More` → `Locate` → whether `Auto-locate to document/mind map position` is enabled
        > 
        > ![Enable Auto-locate to document/mind map position Enable Auto-locate to document/mind map position ](image/5f438fa06bb7171d.webp)
  3. Tap `Document position` to jump to the document location where the excerpt resides

> ❗For cards that contain text, tapping the text area of the card opens editing
> 
> To open the **Edit / Backlink** menu shown above, be sure to tap the **blank or image area** of the card
