# MindMap Handwriting Follow-Along Binding

> 💡**Feature overview for this page:**
> 
> - How to enable automatic binding: quickly annotate near the selected card (fastest) — handwriting written on a card is linked to that card, and when you move the card, the handwriting moves with it.
> - Binding methods: automatic binding (quickly annotate near the selected card, fastest), manual binding, and Focus binding (concentrate on a specific knowledge point for in-depth annotation).
> - Visibility and management: unbind, hide/unhide, and erase.
> 
> **This page is for the following users**:
> 
> - You already use the mind map and are in the habit of writing annotations.
> - You frequently write directly on different cards and want the system to automatically switch the selection and complete the binding for you.
> - You need to focus your annotations on a specific knowledge point.

## 2 Automatic binding: quickly annotate near the selected card (fastest)

> 💡Best for scenarios where you need to tightly associate handwritten formulas, diagrams, notes, etc. with a knowledge point/node.

MindMap Handwriting tool

1. Tap `MindMap Handwriting tool` (shown by the icon above) to open the `MindMap Handwriting Settings` panel;
2. Turn on `Bind Handwriting to the selected card`;
3. Select a card and write directly in the card area.

![](image/a6db6966a999391c.webp)

![Animated demo: writing with Bind Handwriting to the selected card turned on Animated demo: writing with Bind Handwriting to the selected card turned on ](image/a2842401a2660cff.webp)

> 💡The handwriting is automatically bound to the selected card and moves with it.

### 2.1 Write directly on any card without switching the selection

> 💡Best for: frequently switching between multiple cards while writing.

MindMap Handwriting tool

1. Open the `MindMap Handwriting Settings` panel;
2. Turn on `Bind Handwriting to the selected card`;
3. Turn on `Auto Switch the selected card when writing on the card`;
4. Write in any card area.

![](image/1a3858f4b610e508.webp)

![Animated demo: Auto Switch the selected card when writing on the card Animated demo: Auto Switch the selected card when writing on the card ](image/794d96ff79b3abc3.webp)

> 💡This automatically sets that card as selected and binds the handwriting to it.

## 3 Focus binding: concentrate on a specific knowledge point for in-depth annotation

> 💡Best for: thematic organization and step-by-step reasoning.

### 3.1 Entering Focus

There are two ways to enter Focus:

Method 1: Select a card, then on the `pop-up menu`, choose `basic` - `Focus`.

![On the pop-up menu, choose basic - Focus On the pop-up menu, choose basic - Focus ](image/b5f242646e3444f2.webp)

![Animated demo: entering Focus Animated demo: entering Focus ](image/dc4e4a960fbbc39b.webp)

Method 2: In the `MindMap Handwriting Settings` panel, turn on `Double Tap the Card to Enter the Focus`, then double-tap a card to quickly enter Focus.

![](image/12cdaf6144fc7be5.webp)

![Animated demo: double-tap a card to enter Focus Animated demo: double-tap a card to enter Focus ](image/4f49e6cbc1bbb3a9.webp)

> 💡All handwriting created within Focus is automatically bound to the focused card.

### **Exit Focus**

There are two ways to exit Focus:

Method 1: Tap a blank area of the mind map, then on the `pop-up menu`, choose `Exit Focus`.

![Tap a blank area of the mind map and choose Exit Focus Tap a blank area of the mind map and choose Exit Focus ](image/a6102cb9cbde6d3a.webp)

![Animated demo: exiting Focus Animated demo: exiting Focus ](image/3bcdf7f624830158.webp)

Method 2: Double-tap a blank area of the mind map to `Exit Focus`.

![Animated demo: double-tap a blank area of the mind map to exit Focus Animated demo: double-tap a blank area of the mind map to exit Focus ](image/f7788a8b55f02bf3.webp)

#### 3.2.1 Optional: automatically hide handwriting created in the focus after exiting

> 💡Best for: viewing detailed handwriting only when you enter that topic.

Tap `Home` → `Settings` → `Handwriting` → turn on `Hide handwriting created in the focus`.

![](image/41bffeb62725e261.webp)

Now, MindMap Handwriting created within Focus is automatically hidden after you exit Focus.

![Animated demo: turn on Hide handwriting created in the focus; after exiting Focus, the handwriting is automatically hidden. Animated demo: turn on Hide handwriting created in the focus; after exiting Focus, the handwriting is automatically hidden. ](image/a66481e3bb8f9917.webp)

## 4 Manual binding

> 💡For organizing existing handwriting.

Hand tool - Mind Map

1. Select the `hand tool` and box-select the handwritten annotations;

   ![Select the hand tool - drag a rectangle to select the handwritten annotations Select the hand tool - drag a rectangle to select the handwritten annotations ](image/505168abfcd15c6f.webp)
2. On the `pop-up menu`, choose `Bind to card`;

   ![Choose Bind to card Choose Bind to card ](image/e1501ca92a1fe977.webp)
3. Drag the link ball `🔗` icon onto the card you want to bind to, and the MindMap Handwriting binding is completed manually.

   ![Drag 🔗 to complete the binding Drag 🔗 to complete the binding ](image/c70a1f7675fd1c28.webp)

![Animated demo: manual binding Animated demo: manual binding ](image/2a0f42171e1c6c33.webp)

### 4.1 Unbind

1. Select the handwriting area;

1) On the `pop-up menu`, choose `Unbind`.

![Choose Unbind Choose Unbind ](image/fb7d9bc20baabd41.webp)

![Animated demo: Unbind Animated demo: Unbind ](image/2dd2d58cea4ea07b.webp)

> 💡Now, when you move the card, the handwriting no longer follows it.

### 4.2 Hide/Unhide

> 💡Control visibility and reduce distraction

#### 4.2.1 Hide card handwriting

1. Select the handwriting area;
2. On the `pop-up menu`, choose `Hide`.

> 💡The handwriting is no longer shown outside Focus; when you tap the card, the bound handwriting appears semi-transparent.

![Animated demo: hide card handwriting Animated demo: hide card handwriting ](image/a71f2e33afda414d.webp)

#### 4.2.2 Unhide card handwriting

1. Tap the handwriting icon in the upper-right corner of a card whose handwriting is hidden;
2. On the `pop-up menu`, tap `Unhide`.

   ![On the pop-up menu, tap Unhide On the pop-up menu, tap Unhide ](image/53ed5d6c5abbf580.webp)

### 4.3 Erase

1. Select the handwriting area;
2. On the `pop-up menu`, choose `Erase`.

   ![On the pop-up menu, choose Erase On the pop-up menu, choose Erase ](image/53ed5d6c5abbf580.webp)

![Animated demo: erase card handwriting Animated demo: erase card handwriting ](image/c66a91714e44b375.webp)

> 💡**Clear all handwriting bound to the card with one tap.**
> Best for: major content updates, outdated annotations, and reorganization.

## **Common questions for beginners**:

- Q1: Annotations written outside a card aren't bound automatically. How do I link them to a card?
  - Use manual binding: box-select the handwriting → `Bind to card` → drag `🔗` onto the target card.
- Q2: It looks too cluttered after binding. Can I temporarily stop showing it?
  - Use Hide/Unhide to control visibility;
  - Or, in `Focus mode`, enable the option to automatically hide handwriting created in the focus after exiting.
- Q3: What if I wrote on the wrong card?
  - Unbind first, then use manual binding to bind it to the correct card.
